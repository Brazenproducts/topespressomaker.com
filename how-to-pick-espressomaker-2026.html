What NOT to Do

Blind brand loyalty — Your favorite brand might not make the best espressomaker. Stay open to better options.

Panic buying on sale days — Prime Day and Black Friday espressomaker deals aren't always real discounts. Check year-round pricing.

Trusting fake reviews — Look for reviews with photos and specific details. Generic praise is often planted.

The Specs That Matter

  1. Verified reviews — Focus on 4+ star products with 50+ verified reviews. Ignore anything with only a handful of ratings.
  2. Brand track record — Established espressomaker brands have more to lose from bad products. That accountability matters.
  3. Dimensions matter — Read the actual measurements. "Large" means different things to different espressomaker manufacturers.
  4. Return policy — Easy returns mean lower risk. Even well-reviewed espressomaker might not suit your situation.
